6KishBabil Governorate, about 12 km east of ancient Babylon1Occupied from the Ubaid period (5300-4300 BCE) until the Hellenistic period. Patron deity in the Early Dynastic period was Ishtar; it had become Zababa by Old Babylonian times. It was first city to hold the kingship after the flood, ending with the reign of Aga of Kish. It is also the first city for which there are archaeologically attested kings, those being Aga of Kish and his father Enmebaragesi.
